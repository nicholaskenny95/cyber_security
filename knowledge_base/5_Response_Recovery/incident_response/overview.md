Frameworks: NIST RS, ISO 27035-1/2, CIS 17.  
Objectives: Standardized triage, containment, eradication, recovery, lessons learned.  
Roles: SOC, IR lead, IT Ops, Comms, Legal.  
Dependencies: SOC operations, Forensics, BC/DR.
